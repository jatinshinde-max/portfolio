# Product Vision — CGI/VFX Portfolio

## The Core Idea
Five worlds. Each one built. Each one earned.
Not a gallery. A journey.

The visitor does not look at my work.
They pass through it.

## The Promise to the Visitor
You will feel something in the first 10 seconds.
You will not know exactly what you're looking at.
You will scroll further to find out.
You will tell someone about it.

## What This Site Must NEVER Feel Like
- A Behance profile
- A creative agency template
- "Dark mode portfolio with gradient text"
- ArtStation with custom CSS
- Any site where the navbar is the first thing you notice

## The Experience Arc
1. Loader screen: minimal, counted, theatrical reveal
2. World 01 begins: they are inside something. Not sure what.
3. They scroll. The world moves. They are the camera.
4. The world reveals its name. Then its story.
5. A moment of surprise — something unexpected happens.
6. World ends. Transition: they pass through into the next reality.
7. Repeat × 5.
8. After all worlds: the work, sparse and typographic. Then: contact.

## Voice
Spare. Confident. No marketing language.
Like a title card in an Andrei Tarkovsky film.
Not: "I create immersive visual experiences."
Just: the world. Let it speak.

## The One Thing Someone Will Remember
They will remember the moment between worlds —
when one reality dissolves and another materializes.
That 1.2 seconds is the heart of the site.
